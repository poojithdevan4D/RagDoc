How it works

1
Structure Detection — LLM identifies section headers and clause numbering format
2
Clause Extraction — All obligation clauses parsed directly from the PDF
3
Checkpoint Extraction — Every distinct auditable requirement within each clause becomes a separate checklist row
4
Master Checklist — Word document with all checkpoints, subpoint refs (e.g. 2.1 — 3/6)
5
Audit Sheet — Filtered subset based on depth: N min checkpoints or M% per clause, whichever is less
